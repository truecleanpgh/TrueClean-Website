Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

TrueClean Solutions LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, phone number, and email address when you submit a contact form or quote request
  • Information about your property and the services you are inquiring about
  • Technical data such as your browser type, IP address, and pages visited (via standard web analytics)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes for our services
  • Schedule and deliver cleaning services
  • Send you appointment reminders, follow-ups, and service updates
  • Send you SMS messages if you have opted in, including promotions and offers
  • Improve our website and customer experience

SMS Communications

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from TrueClean Solutions LLC, you agree to receive text messages related to your quote, appointment, and occasional promotions. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. For help, reply HELP.

We do not share your phone number or personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., scheduling software, CRM platforms), bound by confidentiality obligations.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
